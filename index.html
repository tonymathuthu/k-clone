I'm a Founder, Serial Entrepreneur, Website Designer, Creator, Health Coach, and Strategist (amongst other things). And I am honored to share my journey with you.

From navigating the fast-paced world of corporate IT contract sales to embracing my passion for health, wellness, and web design, my path has been anything but ordinary. But I am proud to say that building, growing and even closing businesses has been the absolute highlight.

Whether it’s through positive impacts, mentoring businesses with strategy, or crafting beautiful, meaningful websites, my aim is to make a little corner of the internet (and the planet) a better place.

Along the way, I’ve learned the importance of living with intention, the power of healing, and the joy of helping others shine online and offline.

I believe in the power of stories to inspire, heal and connect us all.

My story is made of many chapters; some bright, some challenging, but all leading me to where I stand today. And I’d love for you to be a part of the next chapter. Whether you’re here for business growth, a stunning website, doses of positivity, or just a bit of inspiration, I’m glad you’re here.

So, let’s make something beautiful and meaningful, together.
