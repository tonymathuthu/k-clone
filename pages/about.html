Hey, I'm Kylie!
I'm a girl from Australia who dared to dream bigger, explore farther, and love deeper.

After years managing and mentoring contract renewal sales teams for multiple billion dollar IT companies, I decided to hone in on what I love the most: wellness, tech, design, giving back and breaking boundaries in business. I've studied ALL the things over the years, mostly because I love learning how things work to understand the world around me

Things like getting Diploma's in Business Administration and Business Management and learning bookkeeping, website design and digging deep into software systems and how they work. Or, becoming Certified as a Health Coach, Meditation Teacher, Sound Bowl Healer, and becoming an Advanced Reiki Master.

While I don't use a lot of what I have learned over the years, it's been an amazing experience to apply little aspects of each into unique businesses that bring me happiness and joy each day. Oh, and work with some of the biggest brands, names and businesses all over the world.

That old saying is definitely true:

You don't work a day in your life when you love what you do.

When I'm not crafting up new businesses, pivoting an existing business or shutting down the old to make way for the new, I am spending time with my beautiful family.

My husband and daughter are my biggest fans that are completely understanding of my deep desire to make impacts. But, by far the most rewarding has been to watch my 1 2 year old daughter, Zoe, embrace her artistic ability and start to think of business ideas for herself. Zoe even helped to design my logo using my signature.

Our collective goal as a family is to create our own magic in the world that sees a positive impact with every interaction. It's the heart of what we set out to achieve each day.

And it's because of that, work is never work. It's fun. It's collaborative and it's a joint effort that puts a smile on my face, each and every morning.
