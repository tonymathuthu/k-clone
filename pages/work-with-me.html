ALL THE THINGS I LOVE DOING

Get to the heart and soul of what I love to do.

i love business.
and some might
say too much.

I knew in high school I wanted to have my own business one day. But the thing is, when you have a broad interest range, and a big imagination, it's hard to drill down exactly what you should be doing. And that was my problem.

Jumping in head first without experience didn't make sense. So I decided to become a Sherlock Holmes (not solving murder mysteries of course), in order to solve my entrepreneurial problems.

Over the years, I went into every part of my career with the intention of learning how the companies worked from the inside out. I never treated each job as just a job. But instead, it was an intentional deep dive into learning and understanding.

Why? So that I could reverse engineer the knowledge learned to create my own magic. But also to learn more about what I loved, liked and hated doing to take that with me into my own creations.

There have been a lot of businesses started, stopped and closed along the way, and I love that. Because all those moments in time are what got me to this moment and I wouldn't change it for the world.

So with that said, here is what I've created (and kept) so far.
