Here's the thing, I'm working on 5 businesses and I value our time together. The application process ensures that we work with the right people who need my services. My team will check every application, ensure it fits our specific criteria, and if it does, they will reach out to you with a link to book in a 30 minute chat with me.

Only if you have been shortlisted after your application process to ensure your project is a perfect fit. If your application is shortlisted, my team will reach out to you with a link to book in a 30 minute chat with me.

You can schedule a chat with me via the link my team provide. During that call we will talk about your business, what you need and your vision. If I think your business is a perfect fit for my services, my team will send you a proposal with pricing, process and recommendations. You have 14 days to accept the proposal, and from there, we can begin working together. Sometimes there is a wait as we have existing projects in the pipeline.

This will depend on the intensity of your project. For smaller website work like 1-3 brochure type websites, it can take anywhere between 3-6 weeks. For larger jobs, we might be staging your launch of different parts of your website over a 3-12 month timeline. It is project specific. We will let you know during the proposal stage.

Generally speaking, there is a minimum spend of $10,000 USD to work with me. This can be a long form 1 page website with technical setup such as mailing lists, ebook or content design, graphic design, image photoshop and more. Or that amount could be a 3 page website with 1000 words per page and logo design. It really depends on the business and the work and time involved.
